I am an MSc student in Scientific and Data Intensive Computing at University College London (UCL). I received my BSc in Data Science and Big Data Technology from Xi'an Jiaotong-Liverpool University (XJTLU).

My research focuses on building robust and practical AI systems at the intersection of computer vision, multi-modal learning, and medical image analysis. I am particularly interested in representation learning under noisy or incomplete data, and the real-world robustness of medical foundation models.

Research Assistant · MedFM-Robust (MICCAI 2026, Under Review)
